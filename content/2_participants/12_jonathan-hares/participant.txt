Text: A collection of projects by the British-born graphic designer Jonathan Hares, who lives and works in Lausanne and Zurich. This includes a complex website for the Zurich-based type foundry Lineto; exhibition and book design for the Canadian Centre for Architecture, Montreal, the Bahrain pavilion at the Biennale di Venezia; and a continuing 15-year collaboration with White Cube Gallery designing artist catalogues. The common thread that links all these projects is a methodological approach that aims to favour content over strong graphic styling. Jonathan Hares won a gold medal at the Leipzig- based ‘Best Book Design from all over the World’ in 2015 for “Architecture from the Arab World 1914–2014”, and a Silver Medal in 2019.
