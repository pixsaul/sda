Title: Mathias Renner

----

Subtitle: Still Life Photography Practice

----

Categories: photography

----

Awarded: 

----

Text: Mathias Renner shows an overview of his photographic work from the last two years. Renner works mainly in analogue and focuses on the genre of still life. Commercial commissions in the luxury sector and personal works influence each other and overlap in their aesthetics. The fetishisation of objects, classical themes such as composition and lighting and the question of what constitutes a contemporary still life are the central interests that shape his work. Mathias Renner lives and works in Paris.
